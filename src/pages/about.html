About Le Chat Luthier

Welcome to Le Chat Luthier, your immersive sound companion for enhancing role-playing games, storytelling sessions, or any experience that benefits from atmospheric audio. Our platform offers a rich collection of background music, ambiance sounds, and a versatile soundboard to bring your narratives to life.

Our Mission

At Le Chat Luthier, we believe in the power of sound to transform experiences. Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use platform where game masters, storytellers, and enthusiasts can access audio to elevate their sessions. Whether you're setting the mood with serene background music or adding suspense with dynamic sound effects, we've got you covered.

Features

  • Background Music: Choose from various themes like calm, dynamic, or intense to match the scene's mood.
  • Ambiance Sounds: Layer ambient noises to create immersive environments.
  • Soundboard: Access a library of sound effects to enhance storytelling moments.
  • Customizable Playlists: Create and edit your own sound sets to suit your unique sessions (available upon login).
  • Real-Time Collaboration: Use our socket-client to synchronize audio with remote players (coming soon).

Credits and Licensing

All the sounds on our platform are under Creative Commons licenses. We believe in supporting and showcasing the fantastic sounds available under these licenses. Every time a sound is played, credits are shown to acknowledge the talented creators who made them available. You can find detailed information and links to the original sources on our Credits page.

Why "Le Chat Luthier"?

The name "Le Chat Luthier" translates to "The Luthier Cat" in French. A luthier is a craftsman who builds or repairs string instruments. Our platform aims to be the artisan of audio experiences, crafting the perfect soundscape for your adventures, much like a luthier crafts instruments. And who doesn't love cats?

Get Started

Dive in and start exploring our collection. No registration is required to use the basic features. For advanced options like editing sounds and saving custom playlists, please log in with your Google account.
